SUPPORT FOR EXISTING PROPERTY PORTFOLIOS

REEF can provide a comprehensive audit facility for clients with an existing property or portfolio with façade access requirements.

REEF can review existing properties/installations to ensure compliance with current and future changes in statutory regulation, codes and HSE guidance to ensure the systems provided acknowledge and where appropriate incorporate those requirements.

REEF are able provide advice and recommendation of appropriate actions to ensure client/user compliance and minimise risk.

REEF can provide the necessary technical support to ensure maintenance/examination requirements are correctly specified and carried out.
