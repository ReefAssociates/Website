POST COMPLETION & MANAGEMENT

Following project completion the correct operation and management of the façade maintenance functions and safe operation of equipment and systems is critical to ensure the safety of operatives and those affected by the operations.

There is a requirement on those using and managing the use of the equipment to ensure maintenance and inspection/examination requirements are performed safely and only those trained and competent in the use of such equipment are given access to the systems for use, maintenance or inspection/examination.

Adequate management controls and safety procedures shall be required commensurate with the equipment and operating environment to ensure operatives are managed, controlled and perform the operations safely in accordance with the designers intentions, statutory requirements and local demands.

REEF can initiate and make certain all necessary procedures are in place to ensure those managing the facility are doing so in accordance with their statutory obligations and those required to perform tasks do so safely.

REEF can provide the necessary technical support to ensure maintenance/examination requirements are correctly specified and carried out.
